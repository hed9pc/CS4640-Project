Welcome to our website, where you and your friends can host your own fantasy film league!

How to Play:

Get your friends together and pick your "team" of movies that are set to come out in the coming months. Check back to see how your movies' box office numbers are doing and accumulate the most money to win your league. Two distinct scoring features are available through our site, as well as customizable league sizes, length of season, and roster size.
